What i'm doing

  • Research

    I am committed to developing intelligent systems that can truly learn, even in low-resource scenarios, and are adaptable. My research areas cut across natural language processing, causality, and reinforcement learning. Learn more about my research here

  • Lanfrica

    I am building Lanfrica, a startup that aims to accelerate the development of AI applications in African and under-represented regions, by creating large, high-quality African machine learning datasets and building a data-centric foundational platform to assist enterprises in accelerating their AI applications.

  1. GSK.ai GeneDisco Challenge

    Winner in all tasks (Team: PYCD)

    The GeneDisco challenge is a machine learning community challenge for evaluating batch active learning algorithms for exploring the vast experimental design space in genetic perturbation experiments. Genetic perturbation experiments, using for example CRISPR technologies to perturb the genome, are a vital component of early-stage drug discovery, including target discovery and target validation. The GeneDisco challenge is organized in conjunction with the Machine Learning for Drug Discovery workshop at ICLR-22.
